02
Day 1 · 10am ET

Origin story

Email ▸ STORY
From: RunRec <contact@therunrec.com>
The real reason we built this place.
It started with a problem nobody else was solving.
Casual side-by-side photo of Sal & Izzy on the court — not posed, mid-conversation, evening lighting. Real founders shot, not corporate headshot. Or: hand-drawn graphic showing "9pm CLOSED" on every other gym vs RunRec "OPEN 24/7."

Two years ago we couldn't find a court.

Every gym in Waterloo closed at 9pm. Every YMCA was packed at 6pm. Every "rec center" had four teenagers in line ahead of us and no clue when a hoop would open.

We just wanted to play. Privately. On our own time.

Nobody had built that.

So we did.

RunRec is a 24/7 unstaffed multi-sport facility because that's the place we wished existed when we were trying to find a court at 10pm on a Tuesday.

If you've ever been turned away from a public gym, you'll get it.

— Sal & Izzy – RunRec Co-Owners

PS — Most of our regulars book between 9pm-12am. Turns out a lot of people had the same problem we did.

03
Day 3 · 10am ET

Pure value content

Email ▸ EPIPHANY
From: RunRec <contact@therunrec.com>
The 11pm court hack nobody tells you.
3 things our regulars do that first-timers miss.
Minimalist HTML — no header image. Three small inline icons (one per tip) only. Personal-email feel.

After thousands of court bookings, we've noticed the regulars do three things first-timers don't:

1. For half-court basketball, book Court B or C. They're $45/hr instead of $89. Same hardwood, same hoops. (Other sports use Court A only.)

2. Off-peak hours (11pm-7am) are wide open. The 24/7 thing isn't a gimmick — it's a hack. Completely private feel.

3. Bring your own tunes/own phone to connect to the Bluetooth speakers in the gym. The acoustics in here are unreal. Most people don't realize it until the third visit.

That's the playbook. Use it whenever you book.

When you're ready: therunrec.com/book

— RunRec Team

PS — One more: the door code is tied to your booking. You'll get a text 2 hours before your session with the code. Don't show up early — there's no front desk to let you in.

Behind the scenes · tribe

Email ▸ TRIBE
From: RunRec <contact@therunrec.com>
What happens here at 2am.
A small story from inside the building.
Security cam aesthetic — slightly grainy, timestamp visible top-right (02:14:08), top-down view of Court A with tiny figures playing. Low-key, voyeuristic in a respectful way. Or: photo of empty court at 2am with light spilling out the front doors of the facility.

Tuesday night, 2:14am.

Our security cam picks up movement on Court A. Five guys, in their 30s, running pickup basketball. Quiet. Focused. Nobody trying to be a hero.

They booked the slot two weeks in advance. They've done this every Tuesday for 6 months.

I asked one of them why this time slot.

He said: "We all have kids and jobs. This is the only hour we can play together. We don't take it for granted."

That's what this space was meant for. The 2am slot isn't a fluke — it's the whole point.

If you've got an hour you can never claim back from work or family — book it. The court will be there.

— Sal – RunRec Co-Owner

PS — Some of our most loyal players are the late-night regulars. They book the same slot every week and it becomes their thing.
