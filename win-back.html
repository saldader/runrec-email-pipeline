Day 91 · final · the breakup

Permission to leave

SMS▸ DECISION-FORCE
11:00●●● 100%
RunRec+1 (519) 800-9894
Today 11:00 AM
If RunRec's not your spot anymore, no hard feelings — just reply STOP and we'll stop emailing. Otherwise, court's yours: runrec.co/back -RunRec Team
▸ Tone
  • Permission-to-leave
  • "No hard feelings" — sincere
  • Cleans the list
  • Highest-converter in the entire flow
▸ Why this works

People hate being trapped on a list. Giving permission to leave often makes them stay. The minority that does say STOP improves your sender reputation.

▸ Mechanism

DECISION-FORCE

Counter-intuitive — easier to say no than to ignore. Highest converter in the flow.
